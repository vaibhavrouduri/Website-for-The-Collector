What is Text Collector?

Text Collector is a tool that empowers you to effortlessly collect and manage a series of text snippets along with their respective source URLs while browsing multiple websites or articles. It is perfect for researchers, journalists, and authors who can browse the web and collect information without breaking their flow. Simply select the desired text from relevant websites and utilize keyboard shortcuts to save, access, and clear your collected data.

Important Note: One can collect a series of text snippets (with URLs) from different websites or online articles and paste them in one go into a document like Word, Excel, or PowerPoint.

Features

  • Effortlessly collect text snippets along with URLs from multiple webpages.
  • Organize and store your collected text in the clipboard for easy access.
  • Clear collected data and maintain a clean workspace.
  • Receive helpful notifications to keep you informed.
  • Preview your collected snippets with a simple command

Using Text Collector

Collecting Text:

1. Highlight the text you want to save.

2. Press the keyboard shortcut: Ctrl + Shift + X.

3. Keep browsing webpages and repeat Step 1 and 2 to collect relevant texts and their URLs without breaking your flow.

Previewing the Collected Text:

1. Press the keyboard shortcut: Ctrl + Shift + E.

This will show a popup containing all the text you have collected from multiple webpages.

Pasting the Collected Text:

1. Press the standard keyboard shortcut: Ctrl + V.

This will paste the collected texts and their respective URL's to any text editor you want.

Clearing Collected Text:

1. Press the keyboard shortcut: Ctrl + Shift + Z.

This will clear the text you have collected.

Note: Clear the clipboard after pasting the text into Word, etc., so that you can start collecting new snippets.

For Testers

Want to help test the latest features of Text Collector? Here's how to load the extension in developer mode:

  1. Click the Google Drive Link in the email.
  2. Click the downward arrow next to 'The Connector', and click on 'Download'.
  3. Extract the downloaded .zip folder to the location you want, and name it 'The Connector'.
  4. Open Chrome and navigate to: chrome://extensions/
  5. Enable Developer mode by clicking the toggle switch in the top right corner.
  6. Click "Load unpacked".
  7. Navigate to and select 'The Connector'.

**Important:**

For the extension to function properly, please refresh any pages that were open when you installed it, or open a new tab.
