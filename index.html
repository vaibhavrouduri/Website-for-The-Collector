How It Works

  1. Install the Extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Enable the Extension.
  3. Collect Text: Select text and press Ctrl + Shift + X.
  4. Paste Collected Texts: Press Ctrl + Shift + Y.
  5. Clear Collected Texts: Press Ctrl + Shift + Z.

Installation Guide

  1. Open the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click 'Add to Chrome'.
  3. Confirm by clicking 'Add Extension'.
  4. Refresh any open tabs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I collect text and URLs?

Select the text you want and press Ctrl + Shift + X.

How do I paste my collected texts?

Press Ctrl + Shift + Y to copy the collected texts to your clipboard.

How do I clear my collected texts?

Press Ctrl + Shift + Z to clear all collected texts.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is stored locally on your device.
